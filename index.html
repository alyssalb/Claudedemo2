About

AB Alyssa Barrack

PhD candidate in Texts & Technology at the University of Central Florida, bridging dramaturgy, horror media studies, digital humanities, and artificial intelligence in live performance.

A practicing dramaturg and theatre-maker, Alyssa serves as Dramaturgy Coordinator for Theatre UCF's main season and Pegasus PlayLab while teaching stagecraft and dramaturgy at Daytona State College. Her work spans the theoretical and the hands-on — from scenic painting and stage lighting to scholarly analyses of horror genre tropes and audience-centered zine dramaturgy.

She is an NEH Grant Fellow, Co-Chair for the ELO 2026 Conference Artistic Committee, and a member of the Literary Managers and Dramaturgs of the Americas (LMDA) and the Electronic Literature Organization (ELO).
